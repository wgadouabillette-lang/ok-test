Frequently Asked Questions

How do dealers sign in to the platform?
Dealers sign in on the dealer login page with their email address. After authentication they can access their workspace and dashboard.
How do developers access a dealer's dashboard?
Developers enter the dealer's ID and password on the developer portal to securely access and modify that dealer's dashboard.
What is included in the Starter plan?
The Starter plan includes one workspace, up to 5 dealers per workspace, and full API access. It's free and ideal for small teams getting started.
What is included in the Pro plan?
Pro includes unlimited workspaces, unlimited dealers, priority support, advanced analytics, and custom integrations. It's $29 per month.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Each dealer's data is isolated and only accessible with their credentials.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.
